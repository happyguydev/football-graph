Football Passing Networks

A passing network is just the application of network theory and its analysis tools to understand the performance of a football team in a match.

Each player is represented by a vertex. Each vertex is connected with many others by edges, which represent the passes exchanged between players.

Down below you can check out the passing networks of several important championships. So far we have available the World Cup 2018, Premier League 2018, France Ligue 1 2018, Bundesliga 2018, Lega Serie A 2018, La Liga 2018, Premier League 2017, France Ligue 1 2017, Bundesliga 2017, Lega Serie A 2017, La Liga 2017 and UEFA Euro 2016.
